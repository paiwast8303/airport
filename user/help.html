‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive before my flight? ‚ñº
We recommend arriving at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights. This allows sufficient time for check-in, security screening, and finding your gate.
What items are prohibited in carry-on luggage? ‚ñº
Prohibited items include sharp objects, liquids over 100ml, flammable materials, and weapons. Please check with your airline for a complete list of restricted items.
Is Wi-Fi available at the airport? ‚ñº
Yes! Free high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the airport. Connect to "AirportFreeWiFi" network. No password required for the first 2 hours.
Where can I find baggage claim information? ‚ñº
Baggage claim areas are located in the arrival halls of both terminals. Check the flight information displays for your specific carousel number.
Are there hotels near the airport? ‚ñº
Yes, there are several hotels within 5-10 minutes of the airport, offering shuttle services. Contact our information desk for recommendations and bookings.
What should I do if I lost something at the airport? ‚ñº
Contact our Lost & Found department at +964 XXX XXX XXXX or visit the Lost & Found office in Terminal 1. You can also file a report online through our website.
Can I change currency at the airport? ‚ñº
Yes, currency exchange services are available in both arrival and departure halls. Operating hours are 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily.
Is there a prayer room at the airport? ‚ñº
Yes, prayer rooms are available in both terminals and are accessible 24/7. They are located near the main departure gates.
